WORKSHOP Proposal

Importance of this workshop.

This workshop is important because it will help the people to have knowledge about First aid and develop their abilities when it comes to helping or aiding someone, it will also encourage the people to know these basic things or procedures. This workshop will also provide people with effective, appropriate, and applicable ways that they could apply to themselves and use to help other people who require assistance while we are creating a good connection with them by boosting their character and mindset. This workshop will also help them to be prepared and ready for real-life scenarios or situations and benefit the community with human resources who are trained and have knowledge about these things or give experience to the people while engaging them with the professionals.

Who should attend & The Presenter Qualifications.

The people who are interested in and passionate about this workshop, and the professionals or experts who experienced real-life situations therefore they can share their experiences and give awareness or advice to new learners.

The presenter should have an understanding and experience of the whole topic, the presenter should have the humor to catch the attention of the audience/audiences, the presenter should have an audible voice meaning crystal clear explanation while presenting, the presenter should also have visuals about first aid to aid him in the workshop.

What the participants will learn during the workshop.

They will learn the right process of aiding someone and the things that they need to help someone who is injured or experiencing illness, as well as learn the proper manner or attitude when helping someone and learn the definition of First Aid. Also, Introduce them to real-life situations and what could happen or what are the possible consequences that they can encounter while saving someone. To collaborate or cooperate and build their confidence by doing or performing a collaborative and engaging environment.

Workshop Objectives

  • Understand the definition of first aid.

  • Distinguish the possible consequences that they can encounter.

  • Identify the things that they need to aid someone.

  • Understand the right process or method of aiding someone.

  • Recognize the proper manner when performing first aid.

  • Enhance their observational skills.

  • Build their confidence and collaboration skills.

  • Reflect and Apply their learnings.
