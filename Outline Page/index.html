WORKSHOP Proposal: Outline

I. Workshop Overview

The people who are interested in and passionate about this workshop, and the professionals or experts who experienced real-life situations therefore they can share their experiences and give awareness or advice to new learners.

II. Phase 1: Warm-up Activity

This activity is an ice breaker where the participants (one by one) would have an opportunity to introduce themselves, talk about their reason why they joined this workshop, and what comes to their mind when they hear the word First Aid.

III. Phase 2: Introduction to First Aid

In this presentation or discussion, we are going to provide information or introduce them to what first aid is and the benefits of knowing how to perform first aid.

IV. Phase 3: First Aid- What is the right method or process of aiding someone?

Participants will also be taught on how to aid someone according to the right way or process so that they’ll be able to help without having a hard time doing the real thing or having difficulty.

V. Phase 4: First Aid- What are the things that you need to help?

In this section, participants will know the basic things that they need in order to aid someone such as First aid kit, ADE (refers to adverse drug events), and PPE (stands for personal protective equipment) as well as the importance of the things that were listed.

VI. Phase 5: First Aid- What is the proper manner when performing first aid?

This will be the part where participants will learn the proper attitude and manner when aiding someone by having a presenter or speaker who will discuss the importance of having a proper manner or attitude towards the patient and how they are going to apply it to themselves.

VII. Phase 6: First Aid- What are the possible consequences that they can encounter?

We are also going to teach them the consequences that they might encounter while doing the First aid and making sure that they will understand it or give importance to it.

VIII. Phase 7: First Aid- Individual Activity

For individual activities, we will have an ice breaker, discussions, and short quizzes.

IX. Phase 8: First Aid- Collaborative Activity

An example of our collaborative activity is a Roleplay activity. Where groups of 3 to 5 will get an assigned first aid situation and they have to properly conduct first aid on a patient.

X. Conclusion: Outline topic

This is the time where the presenter or speaker will summarize all the information that they tackled or talked about such as the definition of first aid, the right method or process when helping someone, the things you must have to perform first aid, the proper manner that you need when aiding someone, and lastly we have the possible consequences or complications that you can encounter or experience.

XI. Reflection:

Participants or people will be given a time where they can share the knowledge they’ve got or learned and how they are going to apply it to their life or how it changed them as a person and how they perceive the world around them.

XII. Survey:

We are going to conduct a survey that shows the participants' reactions towards our workshop and their suggestions as to what can make our workshop better and overall more effective.
